Cabbage Patch Kids

Timeline

1960s: Xavier Roberts creates the first Cabbage Patch Kids in Georgia.

1982: Cabbage Patch Dolls become a nationwide phenomenon.

1983: The dolls become one of the most sought-after toys during the holiday season.

1985: Cabbage Patch Kids are inducted into the Toy Hall of Fame.

Georgia Origin

The Cabbage Patch Kids were created by Xavier Roberts in a small Georgia town, where he started making them as a form of art in the late 1970s.

Hospital Section

Each Cabbage Patch Kid comes from the Cabbage Patch Hospital, where they are "born" and receive their unique names and birth certificates.
